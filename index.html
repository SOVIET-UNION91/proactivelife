About

Proactivelife is a healthcare-based clinic that provides therapeutic services for individuals that are suffering from various musculoskeletal and neurological conditions .

We have established partnerships with several multinational organizations to provide physiotherapy services for individuals recovering from a range of conditions, including whiplash injuries, exercise-induced injuries &arthritis. 

Conditions Treated

We treat

Low Back pain

This is one of the the leading cause of Disability Absence from work. This can be caused by by various issue such as degeneration of the bones of the spine, Disc Problem, Sacroiliac joint Dysfunction.
Certain Medical Conditions which cause Low back pain could be serious and would need Urgent Specialist review or MRI Scan.This include Vertebrae Fracture or Caudal Equina Syndrome.
These Conditions are rare and we have experienced Physiotherapists who would carry out a comprehensive assessment in recognising Red flags relating to this conditions and making the necessary referrals to the appropriate specialist for an Urgent Review and Imaging if necessary.

We treat

Arthritis

This is a Degenerative or inflammatory condition that affects various joints of the body.Joints affected include Knee, Hip and Shoulder.

We treat

Whiplash Injuries

This is a Neck Injury that normally happens after Road Traffic accidents due to acceleration, decceleration that causes hyperextension of the Neck. This is normally accompanied by pain in other parts of the body such as Low back, Upper Back and Shoulders.
